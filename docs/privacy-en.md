# Privacy Policy

**App Name**: TimeMap (时间可视化)
**Developer**: [Developer Name]
**Effective Date**: 2026-05-23
**Version**: v1.0

This Privacy Policy describes how TimeMap (the "App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information. Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account information**: Email address and password (the password is stored as an encrypted hash; the developer cannot access your original password).
- **Task records**: Task titles, categories, durations, dates, and other content you create in the App.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device and crash information**: Device model, operating system version, app version, and diagnostic data when the app crashes. This information is used to troubleshoot and improve app stability.
- **Usage analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., session counts, time spent, feature usage frequency) collected via a third-party analytics provider. This data does not contain personally identifiable information.

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect your name, phone number, government-issued ID, or precise location (GPS).
- We do **not** access your contacts, photo library, camera, or microphone.
- We do **not** collect third-party account information (the App does not support third-party login).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

- Providing core App functionality (recording, sync, statistics, export)
- Verifying your account identity
- Fixing bugs and improving App quality
- Cooperating with law-enforcement requests as required by applicable law

We do **not** use your personal information for advertising, and we do **not** sell or rent your task records to any third party.

## 3. Storage and Third Parties

### 3.1 Storage Location

- Your account and task records are stored in cloud databases provided by **Supabase** (https://supabase.com). Supabase is a database service provider based in the United States; your data may be stored in its global data centers.
- Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

### 3.2 Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Supabase | Account authentication, task data storage | https://supabase.com/privacy |
| Expo / EAS | App build, push notifications (if applicable) | https://expo.dev/privacy |
| [Third-party analytics] | Anonymous usage statistics | [policy URL] |

### 3.3 Data Retention

Task records in your account are retained until you delete them or close your account. After closure, your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

## 4. Information Security

- All communication between the client and server is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS.
- Passwords are stored as encrypted hashes; the developer cannot retrieve your plain-text password.
- We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to prevent leaks, damage, or loss of information. However, please understand that the internet is not absolutely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

- **Access and export**: You can use the in-app export feature at any time to obtain all your task data in Excel / JSON / CSV format.
- **Correction and deletion**: You can edit or delete any task record in the App.
- **Account deletion**: To delete your account and all data, contact the developer by email. We will process your request within 7 working days and complete deletion within 30 days.
- **Withdraw consent**: You may stop using the App at any time.

## 6. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with information without your consent, please contact us to delete it.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant updates will be posted on this page and announced via in-app notification. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
